# [Canggu Wellness] Website Proposal: Shopify Edition

**Executive Summary:**
A premium, high-speed digital storefront designed to convert "browsers" into "bookings". Built on **Shopify** to ensure maximum reliability, speed (vital for mobile tourists), and zero maintenance headaches.

**Proposed Scope (Shopify):**

| Service Component | Tier | Est. Hours | Deliverables / Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Store Setup & Config** | **T2** | 6 hrs | Domain, Payments (Stripe/Wise), Notifications, Legal Pages |
| **Design & Branding** | **T2** | 10 hrs | Implementation of Brand Identity on Premium 2.0 Theme. <br>Custom "Vibe" sections (Video backgrounds, etc.) |
| **Content & Pages** | **T2** | 6 hrs | "Experience" Page, Treatments Menu, About, FAQ, Contact. |
| **Booking Integration** | **T2** | 4 hrs | Seamless connect to Booking Engine (e.g., Appointo or external). |
| **Mobile Optimization** | **T2** | 4 hrs | Ensuring 100% perfect flow on iPhone/Android (Tourist primary device). |

**Investment Breakdown:**
*   **Total Project Hours:** ~30 hours
*   **Applied Rate:** $65 / hour (Standard Project Rate)
*   **Total Launch Investment:** **$1,950**

**Ongoing Costs (Estimated):**
*   **Shopify Plan:** ~$29/month (Paid to Shopify)
*   **Apps:** ~$0 - $30/month (depending on specific booking app needs)
*   **741 Maintenance:** None required. System is self-updating.
