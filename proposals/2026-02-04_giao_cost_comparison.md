# Website Platform Comparison: Shopify vs. WordPress

**Decision Matrix for Canggu Wellness**

| Feature | **Shopify (Hosted)** | **WordPress (Self-Hosted)** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Advantage** | **Reliability & Speed.** "It just works." No technical headaches. | **Ownership & Flexibility.** No limits on design or SEO. |
| **Launch Cost** | **$1,950** (Simpler Build) | **$2,470** (Complex Build) |
| **Platform Fee** | ~$29 / month | ~$15 / month (Hosting) |
| **Maintenance** | **Zero.** Updates are automatic. | **Moderate.** Requires monthly software updates/security checks. |
| **Ease of Use** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (iPhone-like simplicity) | ⭐⭐⭐ (Requires some learning) |
| **Scalability** | Good (Apps can get expensive) | Infinite (Code is open source) |
| **SEO** | Good (Sufficient for Local SEO) | Best (Granular control) |

## Comparison Summary

**Option A: Shopify (Users' Choice for Peace of Mind)**
*   **Best for:** Business owners who don't want to think about "tech".
*   **Total Year 1 Cost:** $1,950 + ($29 × 12) = **~$2,298**

**Option B: WordPress (Owners' Choice for Control)**
*   **Best for:** Long-term asset building and maximum SEO leverage.
*   **Total Year 1 Cost:** $2,470 + ($25 × 12) = **~$2,770** (Includes Hosting + Licenses)

**Our Recommendation:**
For a **Wellness Facility** where visual vibe and mobile bookings are 90% of the value, **Shopify** is often the smoother operations choice. However, if you plan to build a massive SEO blog content machine, **WordPress** becomes the better long-term asset.
