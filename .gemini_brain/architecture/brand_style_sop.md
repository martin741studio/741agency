## Color Palette
- **Yellow:** `#FFD700` (Approximate, used for header/footer bars).
- **Black:** `#000000`.
- **White:** `#FFFFFF`.

## Typography
- Professional Sans-Serif (Inter, Roboto, or standard system fonts).

## Component Layout (v4 - "741.Studio Service" Style)
- **Top:** "Thank you for your service booking at 741.Studio..."
- **Header:** Large, bold "Invoice" title (left) and Invoice Number (right).
- **Branding:** No square logo. Use large text: `741.Studio`.
- **Meta:** Date below the title.
- **Middle:** 
    - Left column: "Bill to:" ONLY (No "Ship to").
    - Right column: Grid-style service table (Service Description, Qty, Rate/Cost).
- **Summary:** Totals aligned within the grid's right columns.
- **Footer:** 
    - Website: `741.studio`
    - Email: `hello@741.studio`
    - NO phone number.
    - Bank details (CIMB Niaga).
    - Color nuances (Yellow blocks) in the bottom right corner.
- **Constraints:** Single A4 page.

## Assets
- **Logo:** Removed (Square logo not needed).
